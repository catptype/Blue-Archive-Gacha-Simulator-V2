Welcome, Sensei!

This is an unofficial web application, serving as a Gacha Simulator for the mobile game Blue Archive, developed by Nexon Games and published by Yostar (Japan) and Nexon (Global). An unofficial Gacha Simulator for Blue Archive. Experience the gacha, test your luck, and have fun without spending any Pyroxene.

Features

  • Gacha Simulator: with pick-up rates similar to the mobile game.
  • User account: for tracking information such as collections and achievements.
  • Student Collection: in the current game version.
  • Guest Mode: Jump right in and test your luck instantly.
  • Open Source: A passion hobby project for learning and sharing.
